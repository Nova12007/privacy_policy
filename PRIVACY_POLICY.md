# Privacy Policy — Lakdi

**Developer:** JSM
**App:** Lakdi (package `in.jsm.lakdi`)
**Last updated:** 28 June 2026

JSM ("we", "us") built the **Lakdi** card game ("the App"). This policy explains what
information the App handles, why, and your choices. By using the App you agree to this
policy.

> **Contact:** for any privacy question or a data-deletion request, email
> **shahnawaz12007@gmail.com**.

---

## 1. Information the App handles

We keep data collection to the minimum needed to run the game. Depending on which
features you use, the App may handle:

| Category | What | Why | When |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Game profile** | A display name (you may play as a guest); a persistent **device-generated ID** (created on and stored on your device, sent to our game server so your session/progress can be resumed); and — *(planned, only when Google sign-in is enabled)* — a Google Play Games player ID + gamertag | Identify you in matches, leaderboards, and cloud save | Always (guest) / on Google sign-in |
| **Gameplay & progress** | Wins, losses, streaks, tricks, level/XP, in-game coins & diamonds, owned card skins, your **friends list, friend requests, and chat messages** | Save your progress, run social features, and power achievements/leaderboards | Always (stored on your device); synced to cloud/our servers when you go online or sign in |
| **Online multiplayer** | Game moves and match state, plus the network connection (e.g. IP address) needed to reach our game server | Play real-time matches against other players | Only when you play an online mode |
| **Advertising** *(planned)* | A device advertising identifier, via Google AdMob | Show optional rewarded ads in exchange for in-game rewards | Only if/when ads are enabled and you choose to watch one |
| **Diagnostics** | Basic crash/error information | Fix bugs and keep the game stable | If a problem occurs |

**We do NOT collect:** your real name, email (beyond what you voluntarily send us),
phone number, contacts, photos, precise location, or any financial/payment card data.
The App contains **no real-money gambling** — coins and diamonds are virtual and have no
cash value.

## 2. Local-first storage

Your settings, stats, and progress are stored **on your device** first. Online sync and
Google Play Games cloud save are used so you don't lose progress across devices.

## 3. Third-party services

The App uses these services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

- **Google Play Games Services** (sign-in, achievements, leaderboards, cloud save) —
  https://policies.google.com/privacy
- **Google AdMob** *(planned, only when ads are enabled)* —
  https://policies.google.com/privacy and https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

We do not sell your personal data.

## 4. Children

Lakdi is a general-audience card game and is **not directed at children under 13**. We do
not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child
has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

## 5. Your choices & rights

- **Play as a guest** — you can use the App without signing in to Google.
- **Ads are optional** — rewarded ads (when available) are only shown if you choose to
  watch one.
- **Reset advertising ID** — in your Android *Settings → Privacy → Ads*.
- **Delete your data** — email us at the address above to request deletion of any
  server-side / cloud-save data tied to your account. You can also clear local data by
  uninstalling the App.

## 6. Data retention

Local data lives on your device until you clear it or uninstall. Cloud save and
server-side match/account data are kept while your account is active and deleted on a
verified deletion request.

## 7. Security

We use industry-standard measures (including encrypted connections to our game server)
to protect data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take
reasonable steps to safeguard your information.

## 8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the App evolves. We will post the new version with an
updated "Last updated" date. Continued use after changes means you accept them.
